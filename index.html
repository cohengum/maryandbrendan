Accommodation

If you are from outside the ACT, or Southside (heheheh), the wedding is in the Gungahlin region. We recommend staying at the Gold Creek Tourist Resort, situated in the heart of Gold Creek Village.

There isn’t a lot of accommodation in Gungahlin, so if you aren’t successful your best bet is to stay in the city. If you do stay in the city, we recommend staying at Pacific Suites Canberra. They have a bar and a restaurant on the bottom floor and it’s very close to Braddon cafes and Canberra Centre shopping centre. Pacific Suites is a 20 minute drive from Hall. Discount codes for Pacific Suits are available.

Transport

For taxis and uber please check out visitcanberra.com.au. If you choose to drive, there is ample parking across the road from The Chapel and directly outside of 1882 Bar and Grill.

Pacific Suits discounts

Book via the Pacific Suites website:

  • Use the code ‘WEDDING’ to get a 15% discount on the best available online rate.
  • Book on the first Wednesday of any month between 12 and 1pm (Canberra time), and use the code ‘HAPPYHOUR’ to get a 30% discount to online rates.

Contact Pacific Suites Canberra on 02 6262 6266 if you require more information.
